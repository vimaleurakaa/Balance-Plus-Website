Physiotherapy Services

Irrespective of the nature and duration of your problem, physiotherapy treatment plays an important role in providing pain relief, healing and rehabilitation. We aim to restore your normal daily function, so you can keep on doing the things you love, without pain getting on the way. At balance plus, we have experienced and proficient therapists, who are trained in treating muscle and joint conditions. We carry out advanced clinical assessments and diagnosis methods to pinpoint your problem.

To relieve or reduce your pain, we use a variety of hands-on treatments including passive mobilization, soft tissue massage, electrotherapy, exercise and acupuncture. These all help to increase blood flow to the muscles and relax the joints. During your first session with a physiotherapist, following a detailed assessment, we will identify the exact location of the injury or source of your problem, as well as other contributing factors to it. Afterwards, a tailor-made plan is formulated, keeping your needs in mind.

Dietitian & Nutrition

Nutrition is a key element to good health. Our dietitian nutritionists are experts on good nutrition and right food choices that can make you healthy and fit. Whether its advice on a balanced diet or diet habits to manage the symptoms of a disease or condition, our aim is to change your lifestyle.

Your meal plans are designed according to you goal, food preference and daily routine. Our intention is to make the diet plan sustainable, effective and easy to follow, which motivates you to stay persistent with the plan and get the best results out of it.

  • We believe in wholesome, real foods: no pills or supplements.

  • You don’t have to eat less; you just have to eat right!

  • Our diet plans are not short-term fixes for temporary results. Instead, it is a combination of diet and a lifestyle plan, which you can sustain lifelong, and get permanent results out of it.

If you are just looking to switch to healthy eating or wish to maintain your weight but not sure what to eat, when to eat, how much to eat, etc., then this service is for you.

On the initial consultation, our dieticians assess your present food habits, your goals, your lifestyle etc. and then create a personalized plan for you.

  • We educate you about the food, and how small changes can result in long-term health benefits

  • Our program doesn’t make you feel like you are on a ‘diet’.

  • We focus on bringing a sustainable lifestyle change and not just calorie counting!

Corporate Services

Certain tasks at the workplace often result in problems like lower back pain, neck pain, upper and lower limb joint injuries and repetitive strain injuries of various sorts. Recent studies have shown that around 80% of workers in the IT field are likely to have back pain at some point in time.

Our corporate physiotherapy packages will ensure a cost effective way of reducing sick leaves for the employees and any concerns which may prevent them from achieving their optimal performance.

While anyone can benefit from corporate physiotherapy, there are specific work environments where employees are at most risk. These often include employees who have to travel extensively, sit in front of a laptop or desktop for long periods, have long and continuous work hours, perform repetitive tasks or have manual handling tasks.
