Diet & Nutrition


Nutrition is a key element to good health. Our dietitian nutritionists are experts on good nutrition and right food choices that can make you healthy and fit. Whether its advice on a balanced diet or diet habits to manage the symptoms of a disease or condition, our aim is to change your lifestyle.

Your meal plans are designed according to you goal, food preference and daily routine. Our intention is to make the diet plan sustainable, effective and easy to follow, which motivates you to stay persistent with the plan and get the best results out of it.

If you are just looking to switch to healthy eating or wish to maintain your weight but not sure what to eat, when to eat, how much to eat, etc., then this service is for you.

On the initial consultation, our dieticians assess your present food habits, your goals, your lifestyle etc. and then create a personalized plan for you.

  • We educate you about the food, and how small changes can result in long-term health benefits

  • We focus on bringing a sustainable lifestyle change and not just calorie counting!
