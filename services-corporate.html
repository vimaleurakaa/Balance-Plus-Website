Corporate Services


Certain tasks at the workplace often result in problems like lower back pain, neck pain, upper and lower limb joint injuries and repetitive strain injuries of various sorts. Recent studies have shown that around 80% of workers in the IT field are likely to have back pain at some point in time.

Our corporate physiotherapy packages will ensure a cost effective way of reducing sick leaves for the employees and any concerns which may prevent them from achieving their optimal performance.

While anyone can benefit from corporate physiotherapy, there are specific work environments where employees are at most risk.

These often include employees who have to travel extensively, sit in front of a laptop or desktop for long periods, have long and continuous work hours, perform repetitive tasks or have manual handling tasks.
