About Us

Short Story About Balance Plus Clinic.

Balance plus is a well-established clinic situated in Koramangala, that has been treating clients with exemplary success for over 2 years and has more than 2000 satisfied and happy customers in the list and counting. Never let pain be a deciding factor in your life again.

Our team is made up of some of the state’s most talented physiotherapists and fitness instructors, trained from premiere institutes in India who will strive to help you get back on your feet and do the things you love the most. Go for that hike, take up that dance lesson you always wanted to and get going on that bike ride to Nandi hills.

With a state of the art clinic at the heart of Bengaluru with modern equipment and a calm and relaxing environment, we are here to help you become healthier, fitter and stronger. We are not only looking to fix the current problem, but also to help you prevent future ones. Our therapists formulate a plan tailor made for each individual aligning your requirements with our treatment goals.
